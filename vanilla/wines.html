Our Wines

Award-winning wines crafted with passion and precision

The Collection

A Symphony of Flavors

Each bottle from Château Braha tells a story of terroir, tradition, and meticulous craftsmanship. Our wines reflect the unique character of our exceptional vineyards and the passion of generations of winemakers.

Premier Cru

Grand Vin de Château Braha

Our flagship wine represents the pinnacle of our winemaking art. Produced only in exceptional years from our oldest vines, this wine embodies power, elegance, and remarkable aging potential.

Grape Varieties: Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Petit Verdot

Aging: 18 months in French oak barrels

Second Wine

Les Terrasses de Braha

Approachable yet sophisticated, this wine showcases the same commitment to quality with a more accessible character. Perfect for enjoying in its youth or cellaring for several years.

Grape Varieties: Merlot, Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon

Aging: 12 months in French oak barrels

Blanc

Château Braha Blanc

A rare white wine from our estate, produced in limited quantities. Fresh, aromatic, and beautifully balanced, it offers a unique expression of our terroir through white varieties.

Grape Varieties: Sauvignon Blanc, Sémillon

Aging: 8 months in stainless steel with partial oak aging
