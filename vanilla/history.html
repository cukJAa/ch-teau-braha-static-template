Our History

Three centuries of passion, tradition, and excellence

Through the Ages

A Legacy Written in Time

1721

The Foundation

The Braha family establishes the estate, planting the first vines on the exceptional terroir of the Médoc region.

1789

Surviving Revolution

Through the tumult of the French Revolution, the estate perseveres, continuing its tradition of excellence.

1855

Classification Era

The wines of Château Braha gain recognition during the historic Bordeaux Classification, cementing their reputation.

1920

Modern Renaissance

A new generation introduces innovative techniques while honoring traditional methods, elevating quality to new heights.

1985

Hospitality Begins

The château opens its doors to visitors, sharing the magic of our estate with wine enthusiasts from around the world.

Today

Continuing Excellence

Three centuries later, we continue to craft exceptional wines, honoring our heritage while embracing the future.
