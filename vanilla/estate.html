The Estate

A living testament to centuries of viticultural excellence

The Vineyards

100 Hectares of Excellence

Our vineyards span over 100 hectares of carefully selected terroir, each parcel chosen for its unique characteristics and potential for producing exceptional wines.

The combination of gravelly soil, optimal sun exposure, and proximity to the Gironde estuary creates ideal conditions for cultivating the noble grape varieties that define our wines.

The Château

A Historic Masterpiece

Built in the 18th century, Château Braha stands as an architectural jewel of the Médoc region. Its elegant façade and meticulously maintained gardens reflect the refinement and attention to detail that characterize everything we do.

The château has been carefully restored to preserve its historical significance while incorporating modern amenities for our guests.

The Cellars

Where Magic Happens

Our underground cellars, carved from limestone, maintain perfect conditions for aging our wines. The constant temperature and humidity create an ideal environment for the slow, patient maturation that brings out the best in each vintage.

Visitors can explore these historic spaces during our guided tours, discovering the secrets behind our winemaking tradition.
