Hospitality

Luxury accommodations and unforgettable experiences

Welcome

Your Sanctuary in Wine Country

At Château Braha, hospitality is an art form. We invite you to immerse yourself in the beauty of our estate, savor exceptional cuisine, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Stay

Luxury Accommodations

Our meticulously restored suites offer the perfect blend of historic charm and modern luxury. Each room features period furnishings, premium linens, and stunning views of the vineyards or formal gardens.

Wake up to the gentle Bordeaux light filtering through tall windows, and enjoy a leisurely breakfast before exploring the estate.

Dine

Gastronomic Excellence

Our restaurant showcases the finest regional ingredients, prepared with contemporary techniques that honor traditional French cuisine. Each dish is thoughtfully paired with wines from our cellar.

Whether enjoying an intimate dinner or a celebratory feast, our culinary team creates memorable experiences for every occasion.

Experience

Wine Tastings & Tours

Discover the secrets of our winemaking through guided tours of our vineyards and historic cellars. Our expert sommeliers lead tastings that reveal the unique character of each vintage.

Private tastings, blending workshops, and harvest experiences can be arranged for those seeking a deeper connection to the art of wine.

Celebrate

Private Events

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Château Braha provides an incomparable setting for your special occasions. Our events team ensures every detail reflects the elegance of the estate.

Weddings, corporate retreats, and family celebrations are transformed into extraordinary experiences in our historic surroundings.

"To share our home, our wines, and our passion with guests from around the world is our greatest joy."
— The Braha Family
